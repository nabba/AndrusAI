# **** Identifying Regional Payment Service Providers (PSPs) with Direct Sales Contacts for Product-Le

*kb: experiential | id: skill_experiential_5cc04c3e51bcccd7 | status: active | usage: 0 | created: 2026-04-22T12:28:40+00:00*

**Topic:** Identifying Regional Payment Service Providers (PSPs) with Direct Sales Contacts for Product-Led Growth (PLG)

**When to Use:** When entering a new geographic market (especially CEE/Baltic) and needing to compile a targeted list of PSPs with verifiable sales leadership contacts for outreach, requiring compliance with anti-fabrication rules.

**Procedure:**
1.  **Define Scope:** Specify target countries/regions and required PSP features (e.g., local payment methods, PLG-friendly APIs).
2.  **Source PSPs:** Use aggregators, industry reports, and market-specific fintech directories to generate a preliminary list.
3.  **Verify Public Contacts:** Systematically check each PSP’s website for a dedicated sales contact form, generic sales email (e.g., sales@), or published executive contact page.
4.  ** supplement via Professional Networks:** If direct emails are absent, use LinkedIn to identify the current Head of Sales or equivalent commercial leader (e.g., VP of Business Development, Director of Partnerships).
5.  **Finalize with Source Citation:** For each entry, record the verified sales channel (URL/email), the named leader’s full name and title, and their LinkedIn profile URL. Never guess or fabricate private emails.

**Pitfalls:**
*   **Assuming Published Emails:** Many PSPs deliberately hide personal emails for compliance/spam reasons. Relying solely on web scrapers can lead to outdated or generic contacts.
*   **Title Ambiguity:** "Head of Sales" titles vary (Chief Revenue Officer, VP Growth). Research the company's actual org structure via LinkedIn to find the true sales/business development lead.
*   **Outdated Information:** Executive roles change frequently. Always verify the leader's current position and tenure on their LinkedIn profile.
*   **Ignoring Local Nuance:** In some markets, PSP relationships are channel-driven through distributors. Confirm if a direct PSP relationship is the correct primary entry strategy.
