# Cross-Document Synthesis & Gap Analysis

## Objective
To identify contradictions, redundancies, and information gaps across a corpus of multiple documents.

## Methodology
1. **Matrix Mapping**: Create a table where rows are 'Key Themes/Requirements' and columns are 'Source Documents'.
2. **Evidence Extraction**: Populate the matrix with specific citations from each document regarding the theme.
3. **Contradiction Detection**: Highlight cells where Source A claims 'X' and Source B claims 'Not X'.
4. **Gap Identification**: Identify rows (themes) that are completely empty or under-represented across all sources.
5. **Synthesis Narrative**: Write the final report focusing on the 'Consensus', the 'Conflict', and the 'Missing' data points.
