# Cross-Crew Knowledge Sharing Protocol

## Overview
This protocol ensures regular knowledge sharing between the research, coding, and writing crews to enhance collaboration and efficiency.

## Steps
1. **Weekly Sync Meetings**: Hold weekly meetings where each crew shares updates and insights.
2. **Documentation**: Maintain a shared knowledge base where all crews can document their findings and tools.
3. **Collaboration Tools**: Use tools like `memory_store` and `team_memory_store` to share information seamlessly.

## Benefits
- Reduces duplication of effort.
- Enhances creativity and problem-solving through diverse perspectives.
- Ensures all crews are aligned on goals and strategies.
