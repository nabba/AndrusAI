## Multimodal Content Creation

Enables production of:
1. Interactive ecological maps
2. Animated data visualizations
3. Short-form video explainers
4. Podcast-style audio reports

Tools required:
- Datawrapper/Flourish for visualizations
- Canva/Adobe Suite for graphics
- DaVinci Resolve for video editing

Workflow integration:
1. Automated data-to-visualization pipelines
2. Template-based content generation
3. Accessibility compliance checking
