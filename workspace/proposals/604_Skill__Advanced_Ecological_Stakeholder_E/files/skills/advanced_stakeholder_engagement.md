# Advanced Ecological Stakeholder Engagement

## Techniques
1. **Participatory Modeling**: Involve stakeholders in co-creating ecological models to ensure buy-in and accuracy.
2. **Multi-Level Mapping**: Identify stakeholders at local, regional, and global levels to tailor communication strategies.
3. **Conflict Resolution Frameworks**: Use structured approaches like ECR (Ecological Conflict Resolution) to address disputes.
