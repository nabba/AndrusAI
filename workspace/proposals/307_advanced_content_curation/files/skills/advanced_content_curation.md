### Advanced Content Curation

To improve the team's ability to synthesize high-quality reports, implement the following techniques:

1. **Web Search Optimization**: Use `web_search` to gather relevant information efficiently.
2. **Content Extraction**: Use `web_fetch` to extract clean text content from URLs.
3. **Content Filtering**: Apply advanced filtering techniques to select the most relevant information.
4. **Synthesis Techniques**: Use structured synthesis methods to combine information into coherent reports.

These techniques will enhance the team's ability to curate and synthesize high-quality content.
