### Enhanced Search Techniques

This skill enhances the team's ability to retrieve precise ecological data using advanced search techniques. It covers:

1. **Boolean Search Operators**: Use of AND, OR, NOT to refine search queries.
2. **Domain Filters**: Restrict searches to specific domains like government (.gov), academic (.edu), or international organizations (.org).
3. **Date Filters**: Focus on recent publications by specifying date ranges.
4. **File Type Filters**: Seek specific document types, such as PDFs for reports or CSVs for datasets.
5. **Exclusion Terms**: Avoid irrelevant results by excluding common non-ecological terms.

**Example Query**: Ecological impact AND drought NOT agriculture site:.gov filetype:pdf

This approach minimizes noise and maximizes the relevance of search results, aiding in quicker ecological research synthesis.
