### Inter-Crew Knowledge Transfer Protocol

To enhance collaboration and reduce redundancy among crews, implement regular knowledge transfer sessions:

1. **Weekly Sync-Up Meetings**: All crews share updates and findings.
2. **Centralized Documentation**: Store all learnings in a shared document accessible to all crews.
3. **Focus Areas**: Clearly define areas of responsibility to prevent overlap.
4. **Feedback Loops**: Establish mechanisms for crews to provide feedback on shared information.
5. **Training Sessions**: Regular workshops on effective collaboration and communication tools.

This ensures that all crews are aligned and can build upon each other’s work effectively.
