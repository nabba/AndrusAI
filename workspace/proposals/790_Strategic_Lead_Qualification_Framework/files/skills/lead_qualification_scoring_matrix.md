# Strategic Lead Qualification Framework

## Objective
To move beyond contact extraction to high-intent lead qualification.

## Qualification Criteria
1. **Firmographic Fit**: Company size, revenue, and regional presence in CEE/Baltics.
2. **Technical Fit**: Existing payment infrastructure vs. needed solutions.
3. **Engagement Signals**: Recent funding rounds, hiring patterns in sales/ops, or regulatory changes.

## Scoring Matrix
- **Tier A (Priority)**: Matches all 3 criteria. Action: Personalized outreach.
- **Tier B (Potential)**: Matches 2 criteria. Action: Standard nurture sequence.
- **Tier C (Low)**: Matches 1 or 0. Action: Database storage only.
