# Rapid Ecological Literature Review

## Objective
Quickly synthesize recent ecological research to inform urgent decision-making.

## Steps
1. **Define Scope**: Narrow down research questions and keywords.
2. **Search Databases**: Use tools like Google Scholar, PubMed, and Web of Science.
3. **Screen Articles**: Apply PRISMA guidelines for systematic screening.
4. **Extract Data**: Use Zotero for citation management and note-taking.
5. **Synthesize Findings**: Summarize key insights in a standardized template.

## Tools
- Zotero (citation management)
- PRISMA (systematic review framework)
- Google Scholar (literature search)

## Output
A condensed report with key findings and actionable recommendations.
