# Research Triangulation & Verification Protocol

## Objective
Prevent misinformation and hallucinations by enforcing source diversity.

## Workflow
1. **Initial Discovery**: Identify a claim using `web_search`.
2. **Independent Verification**: Seek two additional sources that are not subsidiaries or mirrors of the first source.
3. **Conflict Resolution**: If sources disagree, the Research Crew must document the conflict as 'contested' rather than picking a side.
4. **Confidence Scoring**: Assign a confidence level (Low, Medium, High) based on source authority (e.g., .gov/.edu vs. blog).

## Success Criteria
- No single-source claims in final white papers.
- All critical data points cross-referenced across $\geq 3$ domains.
